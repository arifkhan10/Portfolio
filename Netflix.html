Netflix Dashboard

  • Category: Tableau Dashboard
  • Client: Personal
  • Project date: Oct, 2023
  • Project URL: Netflix

Summary

The Netflix Dashboard provides an insightful and user-friendly interface to monitor and analyze various aspects of Netflix's content library, enhancing decision-making with its interactive features.

    Key Features:

  • Type Filter: Dropdown for selecting between TV shows and movies.
  • Title Filter: Dropdown for selecting specific titles.
  • Content Details: Displays duration, date added, rating, genre and release year based on selected filters.

    • Interactive Visualization:

  • Map Visualization: Uses Mapbox OpenStreetMap to show the distribution of total movies and TV shows by country.
  • Bar Chart: Illustrates the distribution of ratings for the selected content type and genre.
  • Content Distribution: Pie or bar chart showing the distribution of movies and TV shows.
  • Area Map: Displays the total number of movies and TV shows added each year.
  • Top 10 Genres: Highlights the top 10 genres based on the number of titles available.

This dashboard offers a comprehensive view of Netflix's content library, enabling detailed analysis of content availability and performance across different regions and genres. The interactive maps and charts allow users to explore data trends over time and assess the popularity of different content categories. By providing a detailed breakdown of ratings and the most popular genres, the dashboard helps in understanding audience preferences, guiding content acquisition and marketing strategies.
