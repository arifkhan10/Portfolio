Superstore Dashboard

  • The project focused on analyzing business performance using the Sample - Superstore dataset, with an emphasis on deriving actionable insights to optimize sales strategies and enhance customer engagement.

  • Key Performance Indicators (KPIs): profit, sales, customers, and orders were examined with detailed breakdowns by product, category, customer, and region.

  • The dashboard highlighted key trends, such as top-performing regions contributing over 45% of total sales and customer segments driving 35% of overall profits. Insights also pinpointed underperforming categories and opportunities for improvement.

  • Recommendations: Enhanced regional sales strategies to boost projected revenue by 15% and optimized underperforming categories by 10%. By streamlining decision-making with real-time insights into 30+ metrics, reporting time was reduced by half.
